Peanut Butter Balls

Ingredients Quantity
Rice Krispies 2 cups
Powdered sugar 1 cup
Peanut butter 1 cup
Wax 1/4 of a palette
Chocolate chips 1 1/2 cups

Directions

Step 1: Mix the rice krispies, powdered sugar and peanut butter in a bowl.
Step 2: Roll the mix into small balls. (Works best when put in the refrigerator for an hour or more before making the balls)
Step 3: Melt the wax in a double-boiler at medium heat; and then the chocolate.
Step 4: Coat the balls in the chocolate and place the on a waxed paper.
Step 5: Put it in the fridge until hardened and they're ready to eat!

Chocolate Fondu

Ingredients Quantity
Chocolate chips 6 ounces
Sugar 1/2 cup
Milk 1/2 cup
Peanut butter 1/2 cup

Directions

Step 1: In saucepan or fondue pot combine the chcolate chips, sugar and milk.
Step 2: Cook , stirring constantly, until fully melted.
Step 3: Add the peanut butter, mixing well.
Step 4: Dip some fruit and treats of your choice in the chocolate mix to eat.

Banana Bread

Ingredients Quantity
Butter or Margarine 1/3 cup
Powdered sugar 2/3 cup
Eggs, well beaten 2
Corn syrup 2 tbs
Ripe bananas, well mashed 3
All-purposed flour, sifted 1 3/4 cups
Baking powder 2 tsp
Baking soda 1/4 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Chocolate chips or nuts, if desired 1/2 cup

Directions

Step 1: Grease and flour the inside of the stoneware.
Step 2: In a large bowl, cream the butter until fluffy, and slowly add the sugar, eggs, corn syrup and mashed bananas. Beat until smooth.
Step 3: In a small bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
Step 4: Slowly beat the flour mixture into the creamed mixture.
Step 5: Add the chocolate chips or nuts (if wanted) mixing thoroughly.
Step 6: Cook in the oven at 350 F for about an hour.

Spaghetti Squash

Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti squash's, medium sized 2
Olive oil ---
Taco mix 2 packages
Ground beef 2 pounds
Cheese, grated ---

Directions

Step 1: Preheat oven at 400 F.
Step 2: Cut the spaghetti squash's in two length wise and empty the seeds.
Step 3: Put olive oil on the cut sides then putting them face down on a pan.
Step 4: Cook the squash's for 30-45 minutes, until tender all the way to the skin.
Step 5: Cook the ground beef in a wok or pan according to the directions on the taco mix packages.
Step 6: Once the squash's and meat is done cooking, prepare your squash by filling it with the taco meat with grated cheese on top if desired.

Sticky Toffee Puddings

Ingredients Quantity
Water 1 cup
Vanilla 1/2 tsp
Baking soda 1/2 tsp
Dried cranberries 1 cup
Butter 3/4 cup
Sugar 2/3 cup
Eggs 2
Flour 1 cup
Baking powder 1/4 tsp

Ingredients for Toffee Sauce Quantity
Brown sugar 1 cup
Butter 1/2 cup
Whipping cream 1/2 cup

Directions

Step 1: Butter 8, 1/2 cup ramekins. Bring water to a boil and add vanilla and baking soda, then add dried cranberries and set aside to cool.
Step 2: Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
Step 3: Lightly beat eggs and gradually add to butter mixture in 3 stages.
Step 4: Sift flour and baking powder together and gently fold into batter. Fold cranberry mixture into batter. Portion into ramekins and bake at 350 F for 25 minutes.
Step 5: To prepare Toffee Sauce, combine ingredients in saucepan and stir over low heat until sugar is dissolved. Simmer until sauce thickens.
Step 6: To serve, remove puddings from ramekins by running a knife around the edge. Invert on plate and drizzle with the warm Toffee Sauce. -- OR -- Pour the Toffe Sauce into ramekins and eat out of it.
